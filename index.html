RealCorp Capital ("RealCorp") is an investment and corporate finance boutique focused on European Debt and Equity Markets, predominantly UK and Ireland. We advice on sourcing, originating, structuring, and financing investments secured by commercial real estate assets. We also provide debt restructuring, investment advisory, and commercial finance broking services to clients (excluding retail and consumer markets). We provide bespoke services to our clients based on a sound understanding of their needs and apply innovative and creative approaches to complex business problems to achieve win-win outcomes for all stakeholders.
